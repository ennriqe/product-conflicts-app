Key Differences
• Color/Scent/Flavor or any form of variant/assortis
  - Quality Lines: None
  - Attributes: Wit
  - Reason: Value present in Attributes ("Wit" = white) but missing in Quality Lines.
• Material
  - Quality Lines: None
  - Attributes: ABS
  - Reason: Material is stated only in Attributes.
• Content
  - Quality Lines: Standard tip (A5930), Tongue scrap tip (A5906), Pocket tip (A5918), Orthodontic tip (A5903)
  - Attributes: None
  - Reason: Detailed content given in Quality Lines but missing in Attributes.
• Packaging
  - Quality Lines: white carton box bottom, barcode sticker
  - Attributes: None
  - Reason: Packaging details appear only in Quality Lines.
• Compatibility
  - Quality Lines: None
  - Attributes: Elektrische waterflosser
  - Reason: Compatibility information only present in Attributes.
