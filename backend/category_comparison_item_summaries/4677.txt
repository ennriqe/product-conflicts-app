Key Differences
• Color/Scent/Flavor or any form of variant/assortis
  - Quality Lines: None
  - Attributes: White (customizable)
  - Reason: Color given in Attributes but absent in Quality Lines.
• Light Color
  - Quality Lines: None
  - Attributes: Green
  - Reason: Light color appears only in Attributes.
• Specifications
  - Quality Lines: None
  - Attributes: Noise level <64dB, battery requirement BMS + NTC
  - Reason: Specifications appear only in Attributes.
• Printing
  - Quality Lines: None
  - Attributes: Shiny printing
  - Reason: Printing details only in Attributes.
• Waterproof Rating
  - Quality Lines: None
  - Attributes: IPX7
  - Reason: Waterproof rating only in Attributes.
• Compatibility
  - Quality Lines: Oral-B
  - Attributes: None
  - Reason: Compatibility field present only in Quality Lines.
