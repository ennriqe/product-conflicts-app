Key Differences
• Color/Scent/Flavor or any form of variant/assortis
  - Quality Lines: None
  - Attributes: Black
  - Reason: Value present in Attributes (Black) but missing in Quality Lines.
• Printing
  - Quality Lines: None
  - Attributes: CMYK, 2 Pantone colors
  - Reason: Present in Attributes but missing in Quality Lines.
