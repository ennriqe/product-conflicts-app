Key Differences
• Color/Scent/Flavor or any form of variant/assortis
  - Quality Lines: None
  - Attributes: Blue (Pantone 9460 C)
  - Reason: Attribute lists a specific color while Quality Lines provide none.
• Content
  - Quality Lines: None
  - Attributes: Face Mask, Nasal Mask, Measuring cup, Bottom device
  - Reason: Items listed in Attributes but missing in Quality Lines.
• Specifications
  - Quality Lines: Frequency 60 Hz, Overcharge protection
  - Attributes: Overcharge protection
  - Reason: Quality Lines add frequency information that is absent in Attributes, so QL is not a subset.
• Voltage
  - Quality Lines: 220V
  - Attributes: None
  - Reason: Voltage given in Quality Lines but missing in the dedicated Attributes field.
