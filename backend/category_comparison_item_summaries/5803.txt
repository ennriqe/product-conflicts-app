Key Differences
• Color/Scent/Flavor or any form of variant/assortis
  - Quality Lines: Black
  - Attributes: None
  - Reason: Color given as Black in Quality Lines but missing in Attributes.
• Weight
  - Quality Lines: None
  - Attributes: 536
  - Reason: Weight present only in Attributes.
• Light Color
  - Quality Lines: Red
  - Attributes: None
  - Reason: Light color specified in Quality Lines but missing in Attributes.
• Input
  - Quality Lines: None
  - Attributes: AC 100-240V
  - Reason: Input voltage present only in Attributes.
