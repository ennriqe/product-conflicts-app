Key Differences
• Color/Scent/Flavor or any form of variant/assortis
  - Quality Lines: None
  - Attributes: Wit
  - Reason: Attribute lists color (Wit) while QL has none.
• Size
  - Quality Lines: 72×65×27 mm
  - Attributes: 72×65×27 cm
  - Reason: Same numbers but different units (mm vs cm) – numerical conflict.
• Capacity
  - Quality Lines: 150 mm / 1.5 m
  - Attributes: None
  - Reason: Capacity provided in QL but missing in Attributes.
• Weight
  - Quality Lines: 0.3 kg
  - Attributes: 129 g
  - Reason: Values conflict (300 g vs 129 g).
• Packaging
  - Quality Lines: giftbox
  - Attributes: None
  - Reason: Packaging specified in QL but missing in Attributes.
• Battery
  - Quality Lines: 3V CR2032 battery (not included)
  - Attributes: None
  - Reason: Battery info present in QL but missing in Attributes.
• Voltage
  - Quality Lines: 3V
  - Attributes: None
  - Reason: Voltage present in QL but missing in Attributes.
