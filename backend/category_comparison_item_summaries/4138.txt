Key Differences
• Color/Scent/Flavor or any form of variant/assortis
  - Quality Lines: None
  - Attributes: Violet
  - Reason: Color present in Attributes only; missing in Quality Lines.
• Capacity
  - Quality Lines: None
  - Attributes: 30ml
  - Reason: Capacity value present only in Attributes.
• Material
  - Quality Lines: AS
  - Attributes: None
  - Reason: Material given in Quality Lines but missing from Attributes.
• Printing
  - Quality Lines: with printing
  - Attributes: None
  - Reason: Printing information present in Quality Lines but absent in Attributes.
