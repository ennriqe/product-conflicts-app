Key Differences
• Color/Scent/Flavor or any form of variant/assortis
  - Quality Lines: 4 colors
  - Attributes: None
  - Reason: Quality Lines lists 4 colors while Attributes gives no information.
• Weight
  - Quality Lines: None
  - Attributes: 80 gram
  - Reason: Weight value present in Attributes but missing in Quality Lines.
• Printing
  - Quality Lines: None
  - Attributes: Double sided printing
  - Reason: Printing information present in Attributes but missing in Quality Lines.
