Key Differences
• Color/Scent/Flavor or any form of variant/assortis
  - Quality Lines: None
  - Attributes: White
  - Reason: Color data is present in Attributes (White) but missing in Quality Lines.
• Printing
  - Quality Lines: None
  - Attributes: CMYK + 2 Pantone colors
  - Reason: Printing information present in Attributes but missing in Quality Lines.
