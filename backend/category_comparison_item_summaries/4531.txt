Key Differences
• Color/Scent/Flavor or any form of variant/assortis
  - Quality Lines: None
  - Attributes: Handle: black
  - Reason: Color information present in Attributes but absent in Quality Lines.
• Packaging
  - Quality Lines: None
  - Attributes: Openwindow Colorbox with eurohole
  - Reason: Packaging information present only in Attributes.
• Input
  - Quality Lines: 5 V/1 A
  - Attributes: 1A
  - Reason: Voltage (5 V) missing in Attributes entry.
• Voltage
  - Quality Lines: None
  - Attributes: 5V
  - Reason: Voltage provided only in Attributes; absent in Quality Lines.
• Cable/Connector
  - Quality Lines: data cable
  - Attributes: USB Type-C
  - Reason: 'Data cable' is generic, while Attributes specifies 'USB Type-C'; not equivalent.
