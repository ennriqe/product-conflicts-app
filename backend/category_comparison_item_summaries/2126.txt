Key Differences
• Color/Scent/Flavor or any form of variant/assortis
  - Quality Lines: None
  - Attributes: Wit
  - Reason: Color is provided in Attributes (Wit/White) but is absent in Quality Lines.
• Modes/Settings
  - Quality Lines: Automatic measurement, memory 2×192 sets
  - Attributes: None
  - Reason: Modes/Settings are specified in Quality Lines but absent in Attributes.
